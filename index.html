1 World Venture Partners

Building the borderless future

  • 1 World Studios NYC

    Our team of makers, hackers, academics designers and analysts build companies from our New York-based studios. We are capital efficient and data driven in determining when to build, when to spend, and when to spin out startups. We partner to leverage NYC's talent, diversity, creativity and global presence.

  • 1 World Investments

    We invest in digital, consumer-oriented companies with global opportunity: Innovative non-US startups targeting a move into the US; US companies that are poised for international expansion; and any startup whose breakthrough technology or product has the potential to scale rapidly at a global level.

  • 1 Worldview

    Increasingly, startups will be global-ready at launch, as technology frees them from the burdens, barriers and costs of the past. Under the banner of 1 Worldview, we are building a data-powered framework to guide investment decisions, as well as to foster a meaningful dialogue around global themes.

1 Worldview

What do we mean by "the borderless future?"

For starters, the obvious: Even a small shift toward a borderless, unified global market is a game changer. But low cost, globally connected technology also removes barriers of time and barriers to entry or opportunity; flattens hierarchies between business and consumer, publisher and advertiser, buyer and seller, entertainer and audience.

These distinctions are breaking down fast - and it is just beginning. We have an experienced and creative team, a unique methodology, and a worldwide network of relationships, investors and partners.

Contact us if you are interested in joining us as we build and invest in the future.
